French Ballon D'or Winners

The Balon d'Or (Golden Ball) is considered the most prestigious individual prize handed out in men's football. Since its inception in 1956 by the french news magazine, France football, the award has been won by five different French footballers. Below we see the names of those players, the year they won and the club side they were playing at when they won

Players, the year they won, and their clubs

  • Jeane Pierre Papin _ 1991 (Olympique Marseille)
  • Zinedine Zidane _ 1998 (Juventus)
  • Karim Benzema _2022 (Real Madrid)

Trivial Information

Did you know that ...... ?

  • Michel Platini is the only French man to win the Ballon d'Or multiple times
  • Real Madrid and Juventus had two French Ballon d'Or winners each
  • Juventus have the most French Ballon d'Or wins with four awards won at the club
  • Jean Pierre Papin is the only French player to win the Ballon d'Or at a French club
  • Zinedine Zidane was the youngest French man to win the Ballon d'Or at age 26
  • Karim Benzema was the oldest French man to win the Ballon d'Or at age 34
  • Zinedine Zidane and Karim Benzema are both of Algerian descent while Raymond Kopa is of Polish descent
